How to play

Walk into rooms (Experience, Projects, Skills, Education). Face an NPC or terminal and press E to read. Collect all highlights to complete the run.

  • WASD/←↑→↓ Move
  • E/Space Interact • F Fast travel • Esc Close
  • Cmd/Ctrl+K Command palette (search companies, projects, skills)

Fast travel

      ↑↓ to move • Enter to open • Esc to close
